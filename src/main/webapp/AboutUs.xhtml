ABOUT JOSSH N JUNOON

Jossh N Junoon (JnJ) is venture of JM Services company Private Limited and is launched in year 2013 at Ahmedabad and Sanand simultaneously. JnJ is first of its kind Dance Academy in Sanand and has got very overwhelming response from Sanand. Both the Dance Academies are air-conditioned and capable to impart training to 25 students at a time. Choreographers @ JnJ are young, enthusiastic and well trained. Object of JnJ is to bring latest Dances, Aerobics, Yoga to untouched B towns of State of Gujarat without compromising on teaching standards and quality of facilities.

During short span of time, JnJ has received very encouraging responses and currently has touched 100+ student in just 30 days of opening.
